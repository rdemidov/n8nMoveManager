How mapping works

no secrets copied
1 Create or choose a logical credential, such as Slack bot or Report SQL.
2 Pick the credential reference found in the source environment.
3 Pick the matching credential reference in the target environment, then save.

The saved mapping stores references only: credential name, type, and id. It does not decrypt, copy, or move credential secret values.
